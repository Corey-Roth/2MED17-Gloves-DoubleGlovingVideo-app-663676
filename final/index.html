Your choice matters.

Reduce your risk in the OR. Double up.

Your choice of underglove matters.

A colored underglove can help provide

Safety you can see.

colored undergloves increased awareness of perforations to synthetic outer gloves from 12 percent to 56 percent and colored undergloves provide more contrast between the top and bottom layer when top layer is punctured.

Without a colored underglove

Punctures can hide in plain sight.

Most punctures are tiny and identified only after surgery. Two cream colored gloves provide little contrast between the top and bottom layer when the top layer is punctured.
